Coastline

Fiji has a total land area of 7045 square miles. What is more impressive is its 702 miles of coastline. The coastlines are home to many beautiful beaches and wonderful cliffs.







International Dateline

The International Dateline runs through Fiji. This means that you can stand with one foot in the previous date and the other foot in the present date.
